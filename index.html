1. Service Agreement

UpPipe, a division of Brandnew Studio SAS, provides CreativeOps infrastructure services under subscription agreements (Flex and Scale). By activating a subscription, you agree to these terms.

2. Subscription & Hours

Flex subscriptions: hours expire monthly. Scale subscriptions: unused hours accumulate through the annual contract period. Hours may not be transferred between clients or refunded as cash.

3. Deliverables & IP

Upon full payment, all creative deliverables produced under your subscription become your property. UpPipe retains the right to display work in its portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4. Revisions

Each asset includes up to 2 revision rounds within the AI-briefed scope. Requests outside the original brief scope will be scoped separately and deducted from available hours.

5. Payment

Subscriptions are invoiced monthly (Flex) or annually (Scale). Rates are in USD. COP-equivalent rates are available on request. All prices are subject to +IVA where applicable under Colombian law.

6. Cancellation

Flex subscriptions may be cancelled with 15 days notice before the next billing cycle. Scale (annual) subscriptions are non-cancellable but hours may be paused up to 60 days per year.

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Colombia. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Bogotá, Colombia.
