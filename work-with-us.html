What is a Creative In The Loop?

Not a freelancer.
Not an employee.
A CITL.

A Creative In The Loop is a senior creative who operates inside a structured CreativeOps system — with AI-assisted briefing, workflow intelligence, and brand context already loaded. You focus on craft. The system handles the rest.

🎨
Your craft. Amplified.
You bring the seniority, the brand judgment, the creative thinking. AI handles routing, briefing structure, and workflow optimization. The output is better than either alone.
⚡
Work on your terms.
You choose how many hours you take on each month. Busy season? Scale up. Need a break? Scale back. Your income is elastic — your career decisions stay yours.
🚀
Work with the best brands.
Uppipe works with scaling startups, growth-stage companies, and established brands across LATAM and the US. Your portfolio grows with every project.
What we look for

The bar is high.
That's the point.

We're not looking for volume. We're building the most reliable senior creative network in LATAM. Every CITL in the network makes everyone else better.

Must have
5+ years of senior experience
Not years of doing junior work. Years of making brand-level decisions, understanding strategy, and delivering assertive first passes. Portfolio must prove it.
Must have
AI fluency — not AI dependence
You use AI to go faster, not to replace thinking. Midjourney, ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly, Runway — you know the tools. But the creative judgment is still yours.
Must have
Communication discipline
Clients trust our delivery SLAs. That means you respond in the same business day, flag blockers proactively, and never go dark on a deadline. Non-negotiable.
The mindset
You want to get better. Fast.
The CITLs who thrive in the network are the ones who see AI not as a threat but as a leverage multiplier. They're curious, they experiment, and they evolve their craft constantly.
Nice to have
LATAM market knowledge
Understanding of Colombian, Mexican, or broader LATAM markets — consumer behavior, cultural nuance, local visual language. Not required but valued.
Nice to have
English proficiency
Many of our clients are US-based or bilingual. English isn't required to join but it unlocks a wider range of projects and higher-tier work.

How it works

From application
to first project.

Step 01
You apply
Submit your portfolio link, LinkedIn, discipline, and a short description of what you're best at. Takes 5 minutes.
Step 02
We review
We review every application personally — not with a bot. If your work matches our standard, we'll reach out within 5 business days.
Step 03
Short call
A 20-minute conversation. We talk about how you work, your AI fluency, your availability, and what kinds of projects you want. No tests, no lengthy process.
Step 04
First project
We onboard you to the platform, brief you on your first client, and you're in. The system handles routing — you focus on the work.
